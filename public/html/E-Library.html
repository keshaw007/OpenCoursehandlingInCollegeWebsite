Indian Institute of Information Technology


Library and Information Center

Central Library is a Reading Hall wherein 50 readers can be accommodated at a time, with one stack room and having a collection of about 2357 (books & CDs). The Library has a rich collection of books in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Basic Sciences including Mathematics, Physics, Mechanics and Graphics, Electrical Engineering, Environmental Sciences and also in the field of Humanities and Social Science including English and Economics.
